Our story

“Food made with love is important to me.”

I grew up inthe kitchen, learning side-by-side with my Italian grandmother. The time spent with her fueled my love for cooking as the years passed. To this day when I cook for my family, I insist on using fresh herbs and spices, real ingredients and of course, items from my own vegetable garden. Like most true chefs, I believe that food should be healthy, fresh, and most of all, delicious.
I started making Holiday Biscotti 15 years ago, along with two other traditionalholiday cookie favorites, passed down from my grandmother. I gifted them to family and friends, shared them with co-workers and slowly but surely, Julie’s Biscotti became a much sought after treat. After years of improving and perfecting my recipe, I left my job and began baking full-time. I’ve developed delicious flavor combinations and even a specialty line of Savory Biscotti Bites. Yes, that’s right: savory, cheesy bites of heaven that pair undeniably well with a glass of wine. I aspire to popularize biscotti just as Sex and the City did for the cupcake!
My biscotti debuted in fall of 2013 at various farmers markets and events. The response was incredible so I began offering my biscotti wholesale to gourmet food stores, wine shops, and cafes across the Chicagoland area. However, I continue to develop new and interesting flavor combinations so that I can offer amazing products and change the way we perceive biscotti, one bite at a time. Food made with love is important to me.
