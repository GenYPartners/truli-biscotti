Our story

“Food made with love is important to me.” I grew up in the kitchen, learning side-by-side with my Italian grandmother. Christmas was a time of culinary exploration with almond cutout cookies and snowballs, which fueled my love for cooking as the years passed. To this day, I insist on using fresh herbs and spices, real ingredients and of course, items from own vegetable garden. Like most true chefs, I believe that food shopsuld be healthy, fresh, and most of all, delicious.
15 years ago, I started making Holiday Biscotti along with my two traditional holiday cookies. I gifted them to family and friends, shared them with co-workers, the mail carrier, UPS driver, and slowly but surely, Julie’s Biscotti became a staple in the community. After years of improving and perfecting my recipe, I left my job and began baking full-time. I aspire to popularize the biscotti like what Sex and the City did for the cupcake. I’ve developed delicious flavor combinations and even a specialty line of savory biscotti bites. Yes, that’s right: savory, cheesy bites of heaven that pair undeniably well with a glass of wine.
My biscotti debuted in fall of 2013 at various farmers markets and events. The response was incredible so I began offering my biscotti wholesale to gourmet food stores, wine shops, and cafes across the Chicagoland area. However, I continue to reimagine and recreate my biscotti so that I can put out amazing products and change the way we perceive biscotti, one bite at a time. Food made with love is important to me.

As my son went through school, I made his lunch every day with one special ingredient: each day, he’d find a small note of encouragement on the napkin in his lunch.
